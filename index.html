About Me

MEZHA PEREYRA

Mezha Pereyra is an aspiring Digital Artist. He is working as a freelancer for commission projects given on art platforms or sent via email. He started his career as an illustrator and graphic designer since he found himself interested and fascinated about Digital Painting.

He's been doing commission projects since 2011. However, he's been doing traditional art since he started Primary School. As a freelance artist he make his clients happy and do the best he can to satisfy what they want for the work they have given me.

He also loves his dog Choco, but everyone knows that.

He is currently working on expanding his studio and is planning to make his first book of Illustrations

Mezha teaches Illustration, Design, and World Building on his Patreon!

2011

Started Digital Illustrations

This year, I'm a 3rd year high school student. I was hooked when I saw cool speed paintings on YouTube so I decided to learn digital illustrations on my own. Good thing I have a traditional art as a start.

2013

Graduated High School

This year I graduated as a High School Student at Northern Mindanao Colleges Inc.

2015

Started College

I did start my College at Caraga State University on 2013 but I ended up cutting short midway when I am studying there because of some health issues so I officially started my college life at Northern Mindanao Colleges Inc. where I enrolled after 2 years of staying at home and learned Digital Arts all by myself.
